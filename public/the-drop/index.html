Why The Drop works
🎭
The hidden reveal mechanic
Hiding individual screen times until Saturday creates a "big reveal" moment, a shared family ritual that builds anticipation and makes reducing phone use feel like a game, not a punishment.
⚖️
Parents play too
Traditional screen time rules put kids under surveillance while parents scroll freely. The Drop puts everyone on equal footing. When teens see parents trying to cut back too, resistance drops dramatically.
🔐
Private by design
Screen time is personal. The Drop uses end-to-end encryption so only your family group ever sees the data. Not ClassDojo, not advertisers. Just you.
